Mini About

Oliver is a Sound Designer and Audio Engineer that graduated from DePaul University in 2023 with a major in Sound Design from The Theatre School and a minor in Network Technology from the college of Computing and Digital Media. Through the Theatre Schools rigorous conservatory style training, I have honed my communication, collaboration, and adaptability skills working with a broad range of characters. I have been fully trained in multiple fields of a theatrical audio team including Lead and Associate Sound Designer, Audio Engineer, A2, and A1.

He was born and raised in Indianapolis, IN. Oliver has one cat named Ninja and she is years old. She is a chonky girl and enjoys getting treats and pets from humans. When Oliver has free time, he enjoys doing a plethora of different things. He enjoys driving and working on his car, hanging out with his friends from high school online, and flying the Bonanza with his father!
